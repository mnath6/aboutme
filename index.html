The Early Years

It all began in Chicago, Illinois on a crisp October morning...

I was the first born to wonderful parents who had prayed deeply for a beautiful little girl like myself. Joyful and cheery were my early years, however they were too cold so we soon moved to Marietta, Georgia. Suburbia proved to be the perfect place to raise a family.
