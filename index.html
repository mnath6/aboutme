The Early Years

It all began in Chicago, Illinois on a crisp October morning...

I was the first born to wonderful parents who had prayed deeply for a beautiful little girl like myself. Joyful and cheery were my early years, however they were too cold so we soon moved to Marietta, Georgia. Suburbia proved to be the perfect place to raise a family.

In suburbia, I had many safe activities to partake in during my adolescence

  • Choir
    • Weekly Frozen Yogurt Trips
  • Walks at the Park
    • Shopping at outdoor Malls
      • And of course, school clubs

        • The College Years

        Suddenly it was time for college. Which meant living my comfy suburb for thecity. Well, what I thought would be the city It turned out, Georgia Tech was in its own bubble in midtown! I'm not actually done with college yet, but I'm getting there!

        My Interests...

        I love to sing. I've been in choir my whole life. Yes, even in infancy. I got my scuba diving license before my drivers' license, though the latter was quite the struggle...basically I'm much better in the water than on land. Kayaking and swimming keep me going

        My future

        I hope to live by a large body of water someday. Overlooking one would be fantastic. I don't discriminate - ocean, lake, pond , river..all are wonderful. Will I choose the suburban life? Who knows.
